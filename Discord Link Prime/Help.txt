#Link Prime Alpha A1.0.3 Help

Welcome to Link Prime, Discord's prime method of retrieving Warframe information (I hope).

To start, use [link -name-] and insert the name of any Warframe weapon or warframe you can think of (case-sensitive) into -name-. More features added soon!

#Command List:
  [donate] : Donate to the author (me) via paypal!
    [help] : Shows this helpful menu.
	[info] : Displays information about this bot.
	[link] : Links an image of a Warframe weapon or warframe.
