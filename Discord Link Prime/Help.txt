Link Prime Help and Command List

To start, use wf[<Warframe Item>] and insert the name of any Warframe weapon or warframe you can think of (case-sensitive). More features added soon!
//COMMANDS GO HERE
[link <Warframe Item>]
Links an image of a Warframe weapon or warframe. (Alias: warframe, l)
wf[<Warframe Item>]
Links an image of a Warframe weapon or warframe. Can be used mid-sentence.
[donate]
Donate to the author (me) via paypal!
[help]
Shows this helpful menu.
[info]
Displays information about this bot.
[invite]
Invite Link Prime to your server!
